Our Story

London Loves
a Good Wrap

WrapWala was born from a simple obsession — the perfect wrap. Inspired by the roadside roll stalls of Mumbai and the kathi roll carts of Kolkata, we brought those bold, honest flavours to the streets of London.

Every wrap is assembled fresh to order, using hand-blended spice mixes, slow-marinated proteins, and ingredients sourced as locally as possible. No shortcuts. No freezers. Just flavour you can taste.
